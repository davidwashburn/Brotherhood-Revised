About Us

An overview of the Brotherhood

The Brotherhood are a collection of survivors united against cannibals and those that would aid them. The Brotherhood operates in an organized, militant fashion when conducting operations throughout Chernarus. Priding themselves on communication and coordination.

The Brotherhood maintains a symbolic refuge in Stary Yar named “The Forge.” A nod to their pre-expulsion wings: Fire, Sword, and Anvil. The Brotherhood lays claim to Stary Yar, but frequently patrols elsewhere in the north.
